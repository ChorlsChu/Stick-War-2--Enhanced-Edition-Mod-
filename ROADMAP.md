# Roadmap

These are ideas planned or being considered for future updates. They are not guaranteed until implemented.

## Future Ideas

- Unlockable Enemy Order Bosses in Chaos Levels
  - Explore ways for players to summon or use defeated Order bosses after reaching the Chaos portion of the campaign.
  - Possible ideas include boss summon buttons, alternate training pages, or special campaign rewards.

- New Ambush Levels
  - Add extra campaign-style ambush encounters between major levels.
  - Give more gameplay use to the `Field of Memories` soundtrack.
  - Focus on shorter, atmospheric fights with surprise enemy waves.
